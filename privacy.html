Privacy Policy

The Basics

  • You own your data. That's a first principle of our company.

  • We don't store personal data unless required for the ongoing operation of our services.

Information We Collect

Non-Personal Information. Like most websites, Plotkai collects non-personally identifying information such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and timestamps of visitor requests. We use this to understand how visitors use our site and to improve user experience.

Personal Information. Certain visitors choose to interact with Plotkai in ways that require us to gather personally-identifying information — such as your name, email address, or billing details. We only collect this information when necessary to provide a product, service, or communication you’ve requested.

Visitors can always choose not to provide personally-identifying information, with the understanding that it may prevent access to certain features or services.

How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our offerings
  • Communicate with you — including for support, updates, and marketing (if opted in)

  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations

Protection of Information

We disclose personal information only to employees, contractors, and service providers who need to know it in order to process it on our behalf — and who have agreed to keep it confidential. Some of these may be located outside your home country; by using our services, you consent to such transfers.

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, understand usage patterns, and personalize content. You can choose to refuse cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly without them.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@plotkai.com.
